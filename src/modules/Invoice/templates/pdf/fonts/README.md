# Invoice PDF fallback fonts

The bundled Noto Sans Thai and Noto Sans Lao fonts provide reliable rendering
for those invoice locales. Noto Sans Thai also supplies glyph fallback for
mixed-language content not covered by dompdf's bundled DejaVu Sans font.

The font files come from revision
[`ffebf8c`](https://github.com/notofonts/noto-fonts/tree/ffebf8c1ee449e544955a7e813c54f9b73848eac/hinted/ttf)
of the archived Noto fonts repository and are licensed under the SIL Open Font
License 1.1 in `OFL.txt`.
